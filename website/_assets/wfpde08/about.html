About the Workshop

The aim of the Workshop is to bring together the growing Brazilian community of researchers working on incompressible Navier-Stokes and Euler equations and stimulate further collaboration with the international community. This is the second edition of the Workshop. The First Workshop on Fluids and PDE took place at IMECC-Unicamp, on August 16-17, 2007.

Location

This year, the Workshop will take place in Rio de Janeiro, in the Ilha do Fundão campus of the Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ), at the auditorium "Salão Nobre", located in the Centro de Ciências Matemáticas e da Natureza (CCMN).

Accomodation

Most participants will be staying at the Acapulco Copacabana Hotel, near the Copacabana beach. A van will be available to take the participants to the university in the morning and back to the hotel at the end of the day.
